## 3. Pointer for `advanced-global-intelligence`

### 3.1 Short form
ARL v0.1 introduces a bounded arbitration / review layer for multi-entity disputes.
Its canonical home is the SER stack, where conflict admission, evidence admissibility,
freeze semantics, outcomes, and appeal discipline are defined under witness-backed constraints.

### 3.2 Longer form
As the AGI stack moves from single-entity continuity toward plural, long-lived interaction,
conflicts over identity, authority, continuity, evidence, and lawful re-entry can no longer
be handled through implicit authority or fluent continuation.
ARL v0.1 addresses this gap by defining a bounded, fail-closed arbitration / review layer.
The package is housed canonically in `sovereign-entity-recursion`.
