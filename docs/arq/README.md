## Note on role

These documents explain and extend the subsystem, but they do not outrank the normative documents in `protocol/arq/`.

## Grounding note

Readable does not mean unbounded.
Any interpretation of ARQ still remains subordinate to energy, time, memory, witness, and privilege limits.
